    About Us

    Empowering youth through the transformative power of performance arts in Khayelitsha

    A platform for creative expression and community change.

    Established on 15 of May 2021, we began as a dream shared by passionate young people who believed in the power of arts to transform lives.

    • We provide a safe, inclusive space where young people can explore their talents in music, dance, and drama.
    • Our programs inspire youth to grow in confidence, gain life skills, and create positive social change.
    • Through performances, fundraising, and partnerships, we support community initiatives like school shoe donations to Sinako High School and Chris Hani High School, making a real difference in our community.

    Today, Brothers and Sisters Arts Organisation stands strong, having empowered hundreds of youth and touched the hearts of many within and beyond the community through our dedication to artistic excellence.
